Power Failure

In the event of a power failure, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure all non-essential equipment is powered down.
  2. Activate backup power sources if available.
  3. Notify the command center of the power failure.

Medical Emergency

In the event of a medical emergency, follow these steps:

  1. Assess the situation and provide immediate care if possible.
  2. Notify the command center and request medical assistance.
  3. Administer first aid as needed until professional help arrives.

Wildlife Encounter

In the event of a wildlife encounter, follow these steps:

  1. Keep a safe distance from the animal.
  2. Do not attempt to feed or approach the animal.
  3. Notify the command center and request wildlife management assistance.
